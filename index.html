SWITCHjs Menu:

Conversion/decompression tools for some Nintendo Switch file types

Note: These tools were each made for some of the Nintendo Switch games SMG1 (in SM3DAS), SM3DW+BF, SMBW, SMM2, and SMO (v1.2.0). They may or may not work with files from other games or consoles.

Explorer Explore the contents of files (implemented types listed below, and also zip files)

YAZ0 YAZ0, U8 (.szs, .arc) to BIN (.bin)

SARC SARC (.sarc, .aras) to ZIP (.zip)

RARC RARC (.rarc) to ZIP (.zip)

BYML BYML (.byml, .bgyml) to JSON (.json)

BCSV BCSV (.bcsv, .banmt, .bcam, .pa, .tbl) to CSV (.csv)

BMG BMG (.bmg) to CSV (.csv)
